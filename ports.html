Jamaica is Calling

SeaBreeze Cruises® is focused on destination immersion, and when such a distinct, celebrated culture calls, of course we answer!

We couldn’t be happier to cruise into two vibrant and welcoming ports in Jamaica: Ocho Rios and Port Antonio. With our smaller, boutique-style ships, we were made to dock in these exclusive ports. And as always, when SeaBreeze Cruises® takes you anywhere, we get you up close and personal to your destination. You can get immersed into the culture, history, and vibrant life of Jamaica like never before. And like no one else can.

Ocho Rios...the centerpiece of Jamaica

Located on Jamaica’s northern coastline, Ocho Rios is a major port for cruise ships and offers many beautiful resorts and beaches. This region is home to one of the island’s best known natural attractions: Dunn’s River Falls, which can be climbed. Other special attractions include swimming with dolphins at Dolphin Cove and a tour of the birthplace of Bob Marley and the site of his mausoleum.

Port Antonio...naturally

Considered one of the most naturally beautiful and authentic parts of Jamaica, Port Antonio rests on the northeast side of the island. Take a hiking expedition to glistening waterfalls and hidden caves, go rafting down the Rio Grande River on a bamboo raft, or take a romantic dip in the natural springs at Reach Falls, where waterfall scene in a James Bond movie was filmed.
